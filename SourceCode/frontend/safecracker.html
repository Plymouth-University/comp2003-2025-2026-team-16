🔐 Agent Safe Cracking Challenge

📋 How To Play
Mission: Crack the safe by finding the correct number combination and entering it in the right order.
  1. Choose a difficulty — Easy gives you a 3-number combination, Medium 4 numbers, Hard 5 numbers.
  2. Rotate the dial using the ← Previous / Next → buttons (or arrow keys) to scroll through numbers 0–39.
  3. Press Spacebar (or the on-screen dial) to lock in the number currently at the top of the dial.
  4. Fill all slots, then click Submit Combination (or press Enter) to test your guess.
  5. After each wrong attempt you'll receive feedback — how many numbers are correct and how many are in the right position.
  6. Use Get Hint if you're stuck — you have up to 3 hints per game.
  7. Click New Game at any time to reset with a fresh combination.
Keyboard shortcuts:
← Arrow Left — rotate dial clockwise → Arrow Right — rotate dial counter-clockwise Space — select current number Enter — submit combination
