Agent, Codename NIGHTHAWK is transmitting a critical message from a satellite orbiting 550 km above Earth. Your field radio can receive on different frequencies — but there's a problem. Higher frequencies give a clearer signal, but drain your battery dangerously fast. Lower frequencies save power, but the signal gets too noisy to decode.

Your mission: use the equations below to find the OPTIMAL FREQUENCY — one with enough clarity to decode the message, but battery drain low enough to complete the mission. Target Score must exceed 70 points.

SIGNAL CLARITY FORMULA
C = 100 × (f / f_max)
C = Clarity score (0–100)
f = your chosen frequency (MHz)
f_max = 120 MHz (satellite max)
BATTERY DRAIN FORMULA
D = f² ÷ 800
D = Battery drain per second (%/s)
f = your chosen frequency (MHz)
Warning: drain spikes sharply above 112 MHz!
MISSION SCORE FORMULA
S = C − (D × 1.31)
S = Final mission score
Balance clarity against battery cost.
Score must exceed 70 to succeed.
